About Me

Hello! My name is Roko Peruško, and I am delighted to welcome you to my portfolio website. Allow me to introduce myself and share a bit about my passion for web design.

I am a 22-year-old student currently pursuing my third year at FIDIT (Faculty of Informatics and Digital technologies). Ever since I delved into the world of technology, I have been captivated by the art of crafting visually appealing and functional websites. The way design and functionality seamlessly come together to create a unique online experience is what truly fascinates me.

Throughout my academic journey, I have gained a solid foundation in various web design principles and techniques. My coursework at FIDIT has equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive web design. I am also well-versed in design tools such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, which enable me to bring my creative visions to life.

My Projects

FunkyTail

I designed and developed a dynamic and visually appealing website for FunkyTail grooming studio for dogs. Using my expertise in PHP, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, I created a seamless user experience that showcases the studio's unique services and captures the essence of their brand. The website is hosted on IIS, ensuring reliability and scalability. With a combination of modern design elements and intuitive navigation, I aimed to provide an engaging platform for users to explore the studio's offerings, book appointments, and learn more about the team's passion for pet care. By employing responsive design techniques, the website is fully optimized for a seamless browsing experience across all devices.

Portfolio

I crafted a stunning portfolio website utilizing the power of Bootstrap 5, showcasing my skills and accomplishments in a visually captivating manner. With a combination of HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, I designed a responsive and user-friendly interface that adapts seamlessly to various screen sizes and devices. The website features a modern and sleek design, utilizing the latest trends in web development to create an immersive browsing experience. Through thoughtful organization and intuitive navigation, visitors can easily explore my portfolio, gaining insights into my diverse range of projects and expertise. By incorporating Bootstrap's grid system and components, I ensured a cohesive and visually appealing layout that highlights the uniqueness of each project. Whether viewed on a desktop, tablet, or smartphone, my portfolio website delivers a consistent and engaging user experience, allowing potential clients and employers to fully appreciate my skills and contributions.

oliveGreen

I developed a website for OliveGreen Apartments using HTML and CSS as my first project in web development. Although it may not possess the same level of refinement as my later works, it still serves as a valuable learning experience and demonstrates my early skills in website creation. The site showcases the features and amenities of OliveGreen Apartments, providing potential tenants with essential information about the property. I utilized HTML to structure the content and CSS to add basic styling, ensuring a clear and organized presentation. While the design may be simple, the focus was on functionality and usability, making it easy for visitors to navigate through the site and access relevant details. Although this project represents the beginning of my journey as a web developer, it highlights my determination and eagerness to learn and improve.
